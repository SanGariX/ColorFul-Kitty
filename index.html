Colorful Kitty Sort it out

About the Game

Colorful Kitty: Sort It Out is an adorable and calming sorting puzzle where your job is to organize a group of playful kittens by their colors, fur patterns, or accessories. It’s part logic, part relaxation, and 100% cute overload. Designed for all ages, this game brings cozy vibes, pastel visuals, and the gentle purring of happy kitties while you solve dozens of fun, brain-pleasing levels.

How to Play

Drag the cats into different baskets or areas by their color or features.

Use logic to figure out the right order and space management.

Rearrange if needed — no penalties for mistakes.

Complete all puzzles to become the ultimate kitty organizer!

Unlock new cats and decorations as you progress.

FAQ

Is the game free to play?

Yes! With optional in-app purchases like skins and extra hints.

Can I play offline?

Totally! It’s designed to be enjoyed anywhere, anytime.

Are there ads?

Minimal and skippable. You can remove them with a one-time purchase.

Do the cats do anything special?

Yes! They react with purrs, smiles, or funny faces when you sort them.

Is this safe for kids?

Absolutely. No ads with inappropriate content and completely kid-friendly.
